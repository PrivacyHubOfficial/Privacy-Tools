# Privacy Projects

A collection of privacy-focused projects, case studies, tools, and learning resources exploring the protection of personal information in the digital world.

## Mission

Privacy is becoming increasingly important as individuals and organizations generate, store, and share more data than ever before.

This repository documents my journey in privacy engineering, privacy research, and the development of practical tools that help people understand and protect their information.

The goal is to learn, build, document, and share privacy-focused solutions that address real-world problems.
